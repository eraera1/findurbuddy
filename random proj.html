    About Us

    Welcome to FindUrBuddy – where tails wag and hearts connect! 🐾

    We're a small but passionate team of young developers and animal lovers. What started as a fun coding project turned into something way bigger — a mission to bring humans and pets together through kindness, creativity, and a little bit of magic. ✨ At FindUrBuddy, we believe that every person deserves a pet that matches their soul — whether you're a cozy cat lover or a high-energy dog walker. That’s why we built a playful quiz and cute gallery to help you find your furry BFF.

    💡 Special Touch: For every 100 matches made through our quiz, we’ll feature a real adoptable pet on our homepage to help them find a loving home. 💖

    So let’s find your buddy... and maybe even change a life. 🐶🐱

    Want to support our mission? Every donation helps us improve the site, support local shelters, and spread more paw-sitivity!
